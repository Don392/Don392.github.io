    About me

    I am currently a master’s student at the School of Urban Planning and Design, Peking University, majoring in Territorial Spatial Planning under the supervision of Dr. Zhaoya Gong. I received BSc degree in Geographical Information Science from Nanjing Normal University in 2023 under the supervision of Prof. Linwang Yuan and Zhaoyuan Yu, including an exchange to the Department of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics of Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2021.

    My research interests focus on geographic information science, spatio-temporal data mining, and urban spatial science. The goal of my research is to use state-of-the-art technologies to improve the modeling and understanding of urban phenomena and to advance smart territorial spatial planning. I am particularly interested in incorporating advances in artificial intelligence and machine learning into human mobility modeling and multimodal analysis of urban travel behavior patterns.
